Meserets Recipe

Spicy Stew

I like cooking my cultural food when ever I have extra time, as it take more hours!


Ingredients:


  • 8 cups of onion
  • 2 cups of oil
  • 1 cup of pepper
  • 1 whole chiken
  • 2 table spoon of salt
  • 1 cups melted with spice butter
  • any number of (depends on for how many person) cooked egg

Instructions:


  1. put your chopped onion in cooking pot and cook it until the color change to brown.
  2. add a cup of oil and stir for 5 min.
  3. add a cup of pepper powder and stir and put on slow heat, wait for one hour.
  4. now add your chiken meat and stir for a while and add hot water and wait until the chicken cooked well.
  5. add fresh mushed garlic and a cup of melted butter and leave it for 15 min to cook.
  6. add the cooked egg
  7. finally just taste your stew to ckeck the salt and everything is taste ok for you and enjoy!
